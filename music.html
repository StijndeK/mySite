Global Applied Game Jam Postech Korea

Music for a game, August 10, 2018

Music for an applied game, created at a game jam at Pohang University in South Korea.
For this game jam we formed 15 teams with 4 or 5 students from Korea, Japan, China and the Netherlands.
We were then all given 48 hours to create an applied game about the topic: Dementia.

Fight Music

Music for an action movie, September 14, 2018

My take on the soundtrack of an action sequence from the movie Atomic Blonde.
For my version i wanted to write music to go along with the action in the scene, instead of the original song that was being played over the radio under the scene. Furthermore i wanted to add to the 'retro-wave Berlin' setting.
