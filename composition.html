Global Game Jam South Korea

Music for an applied game, created at a game jam at Pohang University in South Korea. An applied game is a game which in one way or another betters society. For this game jam we formed 15 teams with 4 our 5 students from Korea, Japan, China and the Netherlands. We were then all given 48 hours to create an applied game about the topic: Dementia.
