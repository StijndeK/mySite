Limb Loser

Composition for Game, 10/4/2019
Imagine Film Festival, EYE Amsterdam

One of two games made for the Imagine Film Festival in EYE Amsterdam. As a second theme of the festival was gore, this game is about defeating your opponents in different bloody ways.

The music for this game is mainly focused on creating a scary futuristic setting, while still conecting to the main 'cult' theme of the festival. Creating scary music is very much inside my comferzone, but combining the older cult feeling elements with the modern sci-fi setting was an interesting challenge. I eventually decided to do this by combining different types of sounds and instruments.
