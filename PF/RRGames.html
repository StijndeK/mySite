Limb Loser & Cult Tag

Music
10/4/2019

Music for 2 games made for the Imagine Film Festival at EYE Amsterdam. As the theme of the festival was gore and cults, both games explore darker themes.

Cult Tag

This game is about getting as many people as possible into your basement. The music for this game had to be very clear as most people at the festival would only play the game for a short time, which is why I focused on adding to the setting of and old medieval village with a dark horror theme and supporting the tempo of the gameplay.

Limb Loser

As a theme of the festival was gore, this game is about defeating your opponents in different bloody ways. The music for this game is mainly focused on creating a scary futuristic setting, while still conecting to the main 'cult' theme of the festival.
