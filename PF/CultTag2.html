Cult Tag

Composition for Game, 10/4/2019
Imagine Film Festival, EYE Amsterdam

One of two games made for the Imagine Film Festival in EYE Amsterdam. As the theme of the festival was cults, this game is about getting as many people as possible into your basement.

The music consists of 2 main parts, with the second part being used for the last 30 seconds to put extra pressure on the players and notify them that time is running out. The music for this game had to be very clear as most people at the festival would only play the game for a maximum of 5 minutes. This is why I focused on adding to the setting of and old medieval village with a dark horror theme, while also supporting the tempo of the gameplay.

Check out the second game: Limb Loser

I sadly got involved quite late and there wasn't the time to do the sound design and implement it in the game before the deadline of the festival.
