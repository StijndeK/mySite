ABOUT ME

Hi, im Stijn de Koning: composer, sound designer and (audio) software programmer. Im currently a student at the University Of Arts Utrecht, where i study Music and Technology.

As a composer, producer and sound designer, I focus on music and sound for different forms of Media. For example films, tv, games and commercials. I enjoy making and designing adaptive music systems. As a programmer my main focus is audio. I work on projects like audio-plugins, audio applications and programs that study the relation between picture and sound. For audio i mainly use c++ and the JUCE framework but i'm also familiair with other languages like: Python, Processing, Lisp, Unity (c*), max msp, html/css and supercollider.
